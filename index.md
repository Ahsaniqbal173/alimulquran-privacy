# Privacy Policy for Alimul Quran

**Last updated: April 27, 2026**

## Information We Collect
Alimul Quran collects your device location to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. Location data is used only on-device and is never stored or shared.

## Third Party Services
- Google Fonts (font rendering)
- Gemini AI API (Noor AI assistant — your questions are sent to Google's servers)
- Hadith API (hadith content)

## Data Storage
All app data including Tasbeeh counts and settings are stored locally on your device only.

## Notifications
We send prayer time notifications only if you enable them.

## Children
This app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
